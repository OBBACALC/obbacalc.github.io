Privacy Policy

How OBBACalc.com collects, uses, and protects your information

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

OBBACalc.com ("we," "us," or "our") operates a free tax calculator website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our site, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We take your privacy seriously and do not sell your personal data.

Information We Collect

Calculator inputs: The numbers you enter into our calculators (income, overtime wages, tip amounts) are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. This data is never transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere. We have no record of what you calculate.

Usage data: We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized information about how visitors use our site. This includes pages visited, time spent on pages, general geographic location (country/region level), device type, and browser type. This data does not identify you personally.

Email address: If you voluntarily subscribe to our state conformity update notifications, we collect your email address. We use this only to send you relevant updates about OBBBA state conformity decisions.

Cookies

We use cookies through Google Analytics to understand site usage. These are analytics cookies only — we do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels to follow you across the web. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, which will not affect your ability to use our calculators.

Our affiliate partners (TurboTax, H&R Block, TaxAct, FreeTaxUSA) may set their own cookies when you click affiliate links. These are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Affiliate Disclosure

OBBACalc.com participates in affiliate marketing programs. When you click links to tax software products on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. We only recommend products we believe are relevant and useful for filing taxes under the OBBBA.

Affiliate links are identified with rel="sponsored" in their HTML and are disclosed where they appear on our pages.

How We Use Your Information

  • To understand which calculators and pages are most useful to visitors
  • To improve the accuracy and functionality of our tools
  • To send state conformity updates to subscribers (email subscribers only)
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics 4: We use Google Analytics to analyze site traffic. Google may use this data in accordance with their own privacy policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google Fonts, which may log your IP address. See Google's privacy policy for details.

GitHub Pages: Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect server logs including IP addresses as part of their standard hosting infrastructure.

Data Retention

We retain Google Analytics data for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. Email subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Calculator inputs are never stored.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights or ask questions about your data, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

OBBACalc.com is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at: privacy@obbacalc.com
